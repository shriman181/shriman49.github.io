Intelligence Suite

FIVE MODULES,
ONE PLATFORM

Each module is a complete intelligence product — ingestion, detection, and prescriptive output — not a raw data pipe.

MODULE 01
StructureWatch
Illegal Construction Detection
Compares approved building plans against live satellite imagery to flag unauthorised structures, encroachments, illegal floor additions, and informal settlements. Bi-weekly jurisdiction scans with ranked violation dossiers for field enforcement teams.
Municipal Corps Town Planning Real Estate
MODULE 02
CropPulse
Agricultural Stress Monitoring
Multi-spectral NDVI, NDWI, and LAI analysis at 10m resolution to detect crop stress — drought, pest, waterlogging — before it's visible on the ground. Weekly health scores per field polygon with yield deviation alerts. Critical for insurance claim verification without ground agents.
Crop Insurers State Agri Depts Agri Finance
MODULE 03
CanopyLedger
Deforestation Tracking
Monthly green cover change reports for any polygon — forests, eco-sensitive zones, urban corridors. Detects felling events, illegal mining encroachment, and canopy density degradation. Generates SEBI BRSR-compatible ESG audit trails for compliance-mandated corporates and carbon credit verifiers.
Forest Depts ESG Corporates Carbon Credits
MODULE 04
FloodGuard
Flood Risk Assessment
Pre-event flood susceptibility scoring per parcel: elevation DEM, soil permeability, SAR historical inundation, upstream catchment analysis. 1–10 risk scores per land parcel for portfolio repricing. Post-event damage mapping via SAR — works through clouds — within 48 hours of any flood event.
General Insurers NDMA / State DMA Mortgage Lenders
MODULE 05
LandVector
Urban Land Value Prediction
Tracks leading indicators — infrastructure signatures, construction density, nighttime light intensity, rooftop HVAC density — and trains a gradient-boosted model on transaction data to predict 18-month land value shift probability by micro-market. Site selection intelligence before the market prices it in.
Real Estate Devs REITs & PE Funds Urban Dev Auth
Customer Verticals

THREE VERTICALS,
ONE PLATFORM

Each buyer has a distinct decision problem. Cognivex packages the same satellite intelligence into three distinct value propositions.

GOVERNMENT
Municipal corporations, town planning departments, state agriculture bodies, disaster management authorities. They have jurisdictional authority but lack remote sensing capacity and ground inspection scale.
Reduce field inspection cost by 60–70%. Enforce building codes at scale. Disaster preparedness via flood mapping. ESG compliance for state programs.
INSURANCE
Crop insurers under PMFBY, general insurers with property and flood portfolios, reinsurance brokers needing portfolio-level climate risk data. Ground surveys are expensive, slow, and gameable.
Replace ₹15K ground surveys with ₹2,500 satellite verification. Reprice flood portfolios before monsoon. Reduce fraud. Speed claims. API-native for underwriting systems.
REAL ESTATE
Tier 2 and 3 developers, REITs, private equity funds, and mortgage lenders. Land acquisition decisions worth hundreds of crores are made on gut feel, broker data, and outdated revenue records.
Site selection intelligence before land auctions. Encroachment detection before acquisition. Urban growth trajectory scoring. ESG compliance for green building certification.
